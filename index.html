About Me

With more than twenty years of experience in Sanskrit scholarship and Vedic philosophy, I focus on Advaita Vedānta, Indian epistemology, and contemporary philosophical thought. I truly enjoy guiding students who aspire to explore the depths of our ancient wisdom traditions. Let us illuminate the path of knowledge together.

Areas of Expertise

Teaching &
Research Areas

01
Advaita Vedānta
Systematic study of non-dualistic philosophy of Śaṅkarācārya — Brahman, Māyā, Ātman, and the path to liberation through knowledge.
02
Indian Epistemology
Pramāṇaśāstra — the theory of valid knowledge sources including perception, inference, and verbal testimony across the six Āstika darśanas.
03
Upaniṣadic Philosophy
Deep textual analysis of the principal Upaniṣads — their cosmogony, ontology, and soteriological vision in the context of Vedic revelation.
04
Sanskrit Grammar & Hermeneutics
Pāṇinian grammar, Vākyapadīya of Bharṭrhari, and Mīmāṃsā interpretive methods applied to canonical Sanskrit texts.
05
Contemporary Indian Philosophy
Dialogue between classical Vedāntic thought and modern philosophy — ethics, consciousness studies, and the relevance of ancient wisdom today.
